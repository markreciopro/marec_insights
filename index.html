CONSULTING DNA

What Makes a Great Consultant?

Beyond tools, great consultants bring structured thinking, analytical rigor, business understanding, and clear communication — plus ownership and adaptability. This is the mindset behind every MAREC Insights and HR360 engagement.

CORE ATTRIBUTES (Q1)

  • PF Problem framing: Translate ambiguous questions into clear, testable problems and structured hypotheses.
  • AN Analytical abilities: Work comfortably with data, math, and assumptions — always tied to decisions, not just numbers.
  • BU Business understanding: Connect insights to business models, operations, and financial outcomes.
  • COM Communication: Clear, concise storytelling tailored to executives and technical teams.
  • PJ Professional judgment: Knowing what “strong” looks like and when a deliverable is decision‑ready.
🧠
HOW THIS SHOWS UP IN WORK

Each engagement starts with a clear decision context, MECE structure, and hypotheses — then moves into analysis, interpretation, and recommendations that your team can act on.

HOW GREAT CONSULTANTS WORK (Q2–Q5)

Start by clarifying the true objective, then break the problem into MECE components, form testable hypotheses, and prioritize work by impact, feasibility, and time — focusing on what moves key business objectives.

Translate business questions into measurable variables, identify data sources and limitations, apply descriptive/diagnostic/predictive/prescriptive methods, interpret results in business terms, and validate findings with external and qualitative inputs.

Lead with the answer and what it means for the decision‑maker, use clear headlines and logical story flow, support claims with evidence, adapt tone for executive vs. technical audiences, and end with recommendations and next steps.

Top consultants show ownership, “smart” efficiency, adaptability, collaboration, continuous learning, and leadership — treating the client’s problem as their own and improving frameworks and tools over time.

In practice, this means MAREC isn’t just building dashboards — it’s acting as a strategic data and mission intelligence partner for your organization.
